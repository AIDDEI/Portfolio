Strings Attached

Step into the surreal world of Strings Attached, a hand crafted 2D mobile game!

You play as Pips, a puppet-like circus performer who finds himself hanging awake on an endless tightrope in a bizarre circus tent. The circusmaster's haunting whisper urges him to dance forever. As strange objects shower down and props tumble over the rope, Pips must dodge, jump, hang, and dash to survive - and break free from the endless performance before he becomes just another puppet in the show.

Tilt your device to walk the rope, swipe up to jump, swipe down to hang, hold to crouch and swipe sideways to dash through danger. Every meter walked brings you closer to uncovering fragments of the characters' backstories hidden within the circus.

Unlock new performers and experience their personal journeys:
- Toots, a young girl seeking the attention she never received at home, joins the circus in search of appreciation - yet still yearns for her parents' love.
- Daps, a professional clown who can't stand the silence after his failed jokes, decides to start anew as a tightrope walker. Protective and kind-hearted, he looks after Toots as if she were his own daughter.

Earn tickets to unlock new characters, and reveal the dark whimsical stories behind each performer. Can you walk the rope long enough to set them free - or will you become part of the circusmaster's eternal circus?

C# Unity Mobile

Project Context

During the CMI Game Minor, I worked together with four other students to develop a 2D Unity game for mobile devices. We had six weeks to build a fully playable game from scratch. The result of this project is our game Strings Attached!

My Role

I was the sole developer on the team, which meant I programmed all the game logic and functionality in C#. In addition, I ensured that thorough documentation was created, covering the code structure, version control workflow, and user stories. I also handled the sound design for the game.
