Battle Bot

For this project, I built a fully functioning Battle Bot powered by a microcontroller and controlled using JavaScript. The goal was to design, program and optimize a robot capable of competing in a tournament against the Battle Bots created by fellow students.

I developed the bot's behavoir, movement logic, and combat strategies through custom JavaScript code running on the microcontroller. This involved creating responsive logic, tuning the bot's speed and maneuverability, and ensuring reliable performance during matches.

My Battle Bot successfully advanced through the initial rounds and reached the round of 16, demonstrating effective strategy, stability, and strong overall performance in the competition.
